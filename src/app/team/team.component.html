Meet the Founder

Founder & CEO

Aishwarya Amuda (She/her/hers)

Driven by a deep passion for education and social change, I founded Social XP to make learning essential social skills accessible and engaging. After graduating from Harvard Business School in May 2024, I committed full-time to building this platform, combining my experiences from Vedantu , a Series E EdTech startup, DC Advisory as an EdTech investment banker, and as an EdTech investor at GSV Ventures .

As a first-generation college graduate from IIT Bombay (Bachelors in Engineering) , I’ve seen firsthand the power of education to create systemic change. My career has always revolved around blending technology with learning, and Social XP is a natural evolution of that mission—using gamification to address the critical gap in social-emotional learning.
